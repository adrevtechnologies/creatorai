# Content Strategy Guide

Proven formulas and templates for generating viral content across platforms.

## Content Hook Formulas (7 Proven Types)

### 1. **The Curiosity Gap Hook**

_Creates intrigue by hiding the payoff_

**Template:**

- "You've been doing [TASK] wrong your whole life"
- "Nobody talks about this [THING] but it's true"
- "This [PRODUCT] hack will change everything"

**Best for:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

**Example:**

```
Hook: "You've been brushing your teeth wrong your whole life"
Body: [explain correct technique with visuals]
CTA: "Try this tonight and let me know if it changed your life"
```

**Prompt for AI:**

```
Generate a curiosity gap hook about [TOPIC] in 10 words or less.
Follow pattern: "You've been doing [COMMON_ACTION] wrongly but..."
Make it specific and clickable. Output only the hook.
```

---

### 2. **The Pattern Interrupt Hook**

_Breaks scrolling momentum with surprise_

**Template:**

- "[UNEXPECTED_THING] just happened and I had to share"
- "Wait for the end..." (then subvert expectations)
- "This will hurt to watch but you need to see it"

**Best for:** All platforms

**Example:**

```
Hook: "This AI just did something I wasn't expecting"
Body: [show unexpected AI capability with context]
CTA: "What would you do with this? Comment below"
```

---

### 3. **The Authority/Expert Hook**

_Position as someone who knows_

**Template:**

- "As a [ROLE], here's what most people get wrong about [TOPIC]"
- "[YEARS] of [EXPERIENCE], and this is the #1 mistake"
- "I tested [X] methods and only 1 worked"

**Best for:** Educational, Professional audiences (YouTube, LinkedIn)

**Example:**

```
Hook: "As an AI researcher, here's what nobody tells you about ChatGPT"
Body: [2-3 technical insights explained simply]
CTA: "Subscribe for more insider tech knowledge"
```

---

### 4. **The Trend/Zeitgeist Hook**

_Ride current waves_

**Template:**

- "[TRENDING_THING] is everywhere and here's why"
- "Everyone's talking about [TREND] but missing [ANGLE]"
- "[CELEBRITY/PUBLIC_FIGURE] just [ACTION] and here's what it means"

**Best for:** TikTok, Twitter/X, News angles

**Example:**

```
Hook: "ChatGPT's new feature just changed the game and nobody's talking about it"
Body: [explain technical feature in simple terms]
CTA: "What feature do YOU want to see next?"
```

---

### 5. **The Demographic/Relatable Hook**

_Speak directly to an identity_

**Template:**

- "[DEMOGRAPHIC] when [SITUATION]"
- "If you're a [TYPE_OF_PERSON], this is for you"
- "[DEMOGRAPHIC] be like..." (humor angle)

**Best for:** TikTok, Instagram, Twitter

**Example:**

```
Hook: "Developers in 2024 be like..."
Body: [relatable coding scenarios as quick cuts]
CTA: "Send this to your dev teammates"
```

---

### 6. **The Transformation Hook**

_Promise result/change_

**Template:**

- "I did [CHALLENGE] for [TIMEFRAME] and here's what happened"
- "This [Method] took me from [BEFORE] to [AFTER] in [TIME]"
- "[OUTCOME] in [X] days using this [METHOD]"

**Best for:** Educational, Self-improvement, How-to

**Example:**

```
Hook: "I learned AI in 30 days. Here's what I built."
Body: [step-by-step process, resources, mistakes]
CTA: "Want to learn? Start here [link]"
```

---

### 7. **The Narrative/Story Hook**

_Tell a compelling story_

**Template:**

- "Here's a story about [SITUATION]..."
- "This happened to me yesterday and it blew my mind"
- "A guy just texted me and said [THING]"

**Best for:** All platforms

**Example:**

```
Hook: "We deployed an AI model wrong and lost $50K. Here's what we learned."
Body: [tell the story of what went wrong and why]
CTA: "Don't make this mistake. Share with your team."
```

## Niche-Specific Content Strategies

### Niche 1: Motivational/Self-Improvement

**Target Audience:** 18-35, ambitious, scrolling on break

**Proven Hooks:**

- "Your future self is watching you right now"
- "This habit changed my entire year"
- "The richest people do this daily"

**Content Structure (60 seconds):**

```
0-5s:  Hook + eye contact or shocking visual
5-30s: 2-3 actionable insights or story
30-50s: Application or transformation
50-60s: CTA + emotional resonance
```

**Platform Strategy:**

- **TikTok:** Very trending sounds, repeat hook 2x
- **Instagram:** Clean aesthetic, subtitles, profile link
- **YouTube Shorts:** Longer form (30-90s), more detail
- **LinkedIn:** Professional angle, authentic tone

**Hook Templates (Generated Examples):**

```
"The reason you're not progressing is simpler than you think..."
"Top 1% people never tell you this about consistency..."
"I wasted 3 years before discovering this one thing..."
```

---

### Niche 2: AI/Tech Trends

**Target Audience:** 20-40, tech-savvy, investors, professionals

**Proven hooks:**

- " [NEW_AI_TOOL] just launched and it's insane"
- "This AI feature will take your job (here's how to prepare)"
- "Developers hate this one simple trick"

**Content Structure:**

```
0-5s:  Hook + quick demo or shocking clip
5-25s: Explanation of what and why it matters
25-45s: How to use it / Real-world impact
45-60s: Future implications or call to action
```

**Research Sources:**

- Product Hunt (daily)
- Twitter/X #AI #ml threads
- HackerNews
- YouTube tech channels
- Arxiv papers (preprints)

**Hook Templates:**

```
"Anthropic's new Claude feature is breaking the internet..."
"This Python library will revolutionize your workflow..."
"The death of Google is happening and nobody noticed..."
```

---

### Niche 3: Educational/How-To

**Target Audience:** Students, professionals wanting to learn

**Proven Hooks:**

- "The fastest way to learn [SKILL] in [TIMEFRAME]"
- "This method is used by [elite_group]"
- "Here's the 3-step process [experts] don't share"

**Content Structure:**

```
0-5s:  Hook + promise of outcome
5-15s: Step 1 with visuals/demo
15-30s: Step 2 with tips
30-45s: Step 3 with common mistake
45-55s: Summary + result shown
55-60s: CTA - where to learn more
```

**Hook Templates:**

```
"Master Python in 30 minutes..."
"The one tool every programmer should know..."
"This shortcut saves 2 hours daily..."
```

---

### Niche 4: Entertainment/Funny

**Target Audience:** General, 13-25, looking for laughs

**Proven Hooks:**

- "[GROUP] when [SITUATION]" (relatable humor)
- "POV: You're a [CHARACTER]" (perspective)
- "This is the most [ADJECTIVE] thing..."

**Content Structure:**

```
0-2s:  Hook/Setup
2-40s: Building tension/punchlines
40-50s: Multiple quick laughs
50-60s: Payoff/End scene
```

**Hook Templates:**

```
"POV: You're trying to learn math..."
"When your code finally works..."
"Tech bros explaining their startup..."
```

---

### Niche 5: News/Commentary

**Target Audience:** Informed, curious, social

**Proven Hooks:**

- "[NEWS] just happened and here's what you missed"
- "Everyone's wrong about [TOPIC]"
- "[Public_figure] just said [controversial_thing]"

**Research/Sourcing:**

- Twitter trending
- News APIs (NewsAPI, etc.)
- Reddit /r/news
- Hacker News
- YouTube news channels

**Hook Templates:**

```
"The media won't tell you this about the latest AI ban..."
"Here's what really happened with [company] stocks..."
```

## A/B Testing Framework

### What to Test

1. **Hook Variations (First 3 seconds)**
   - Curiosity gap vs. Direct
   - Question vs. Statement
   - Emotional vs. Logical

2. **Visuals**
   - Colorful vs. Minimal
   - Text overlay vs. No text
   - Faces vs. Objects

3. **Audio**
   - Trending audio vs. Unique
   - Voiceover vs. Music only
   - Fast cuts vs. Smooth transitions

4. **Posting Times**
   - 9 AM (morning scroll)
   - 3 PM (afternoon break)
   - 9 PM (evening entertainment)
   - 12 AM (night owls)

### Tracking Metrics (First 24 Hours)

| Metric          | Target               | Winner                   |
| --------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Views           | Compare all variants | Highest views            |
| Engagement Rate | 5-8%                 | Likes + comments / views |
| Watch%          | 40%+                 | Avg. timeline % watched  |
| Shares          | >2% of views         | Best for viral           |
| Saves           | >1% of views         | High-value content       |

## Prompt Templates for AI Generation

### Script Generation

```
Generate a 60-second TikTok script for the "{NICHE}" niche on the topic "{TOPIC}".

Hook (must be compelling and clickable):
Use this structure: "{HOOK_PATTERN}"

Make it about 5-10 words.

Main Body (35-45 seconds of spoken content):
- Start with hook
- Include 2-3 surprising insights or steps
- Use simple language
- Build to a payoff

Call to Action (5 seconds):
- Make it organic (not "smash the like button")
- Encourage sharing or commenting

Requirements:
- Spoken tone: {TONE}
- Audience: {AUDIENCE}
- Use "you" to address viewer directly
- Create urgency or emotional connection
- Include one specific, actionable insight

Output format:
HOOK: [text]
BODY: [text]
CTA: [text]
```

### Caption Generation

```
Create captions for {PLATFORM} for a {TOPIC} video.

Hook: {HOOK_TEXT}

Requirements:
- Length: Platform-appropriate
- Include relevant hashtags for {PLATFORM}
- Encourage engagement (ask question/invite comment)
- On-brand tone: {TONE}

include:
- Primary caption (100-150 chars)
- Hashtag mix (trending + niche-specific)
- Optional: Emoji use
- Call-to-action

Platforms:
- TikTok: 2200 char limit, 35 hashtags max
- Instagram: 2200 char limit, 30 hashtags max
- Twitter: 280 chars, 3-5 hashtags
- YouTube: 5000 chars, natural language
- LinkedIn: 3000 chars, professional, 5-10 hashtags
```
